Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to all individuals who interact with GateMate, including:

  • Society Chairmen — who register a society and manage the platform via the web dashboard
  • Members / Residents — who receive WhatsApp gate alerts and reply to approve or reject entry
  • Watchmen — who receive a WhatsApp invitation and send gate commands via WhatsApp

This policy covers all personal data processed through the GateMate web application, backend services, and WhatsApp messaging via Meta's WhatsApp Business Cloud API.

WhatsApp Cloud API — Meta's Role

GateMate uses Meta's WhatsApp Business Cloud API to deliver gate alert messages. This is a business-grade API — not a personal WhatsApp account — and is the method recommended by Meta for privacy-compliant business messaging.

6.1 What This Means for Your Data

  • When we send a WhatsApp message to your number, your phone number and message content are transmitted to Meta's servers for delivery
  • Meta processes this data as our Data Processor under our instructions, as defined in the WhatsApp Business Terms of Service
  • Meta's own Privacy Policy governs their processing of your data. We are not responsible for Meta's data practices
  • Message content between you and GateMate's bot number is end-to-end encrypted in transit
  • We do not use WhatsApp message data for any purpose other than delivering gate management notifications and receiving your YES/NO response

6.2 Your WhatsApp Opt-In and Opt-Out

  • Opt-in: You provide consent by replying ACCEPT to your WhatsApp invite. Until you do, no further messages are sent
  • Opt-out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from our bot, or by asking your society chairman to remove you from the platform
  • You may also block the GateMate WhatsApp number directly in your WhatsApp app at any time
  • Once removed or opted out, we stop all messaging to your number within 24 hours

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal data, consistent with the DPDP Rules 2025 reasonable safeguards requirement:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a minimum of 10 salt rounds — plain text passwords are never stored
  • API authentication uses signed JWT tokens with a 7-day expiry
  • All API endpoints that access society data require a valid JWT and return data only for the authenticated chairman's society
  • WhatsApp messages are transmitted via Meta's end-to-end encrypted infrastructure
  • Database access is restricted to the application server — no direct public access
  • HTTPS is enforced for all web dashboard and API communication
  • Invite tokens are single-use, UUID-generated, and expire within 48 hours
  • Removed members and watchmen are soft-deleted — their records are isolated from active queries immediately
🔔
Breach Notification
In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify affected individuals and the Data Protection Board of India (DPBI) as required under the DPDP Act 2023 and DPDP Rules 2025. Notification will be sent without undue delay and within the timeframe prescribed by the Board.

Children's Data

GateMate is intended for use by adults only. Our service is designed for housing society management and targets adults aged 18 and above.

Cookies and Tracking

GateMate's web dashboard uses minimal, essential cookies only:
